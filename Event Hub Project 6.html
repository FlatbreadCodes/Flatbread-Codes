Promise Culinary School in Person Open House

Promise Culinary School is part of Elijah’s Promise, a community-based non-profit, that harnesses the power of food by providing social service assistance, and over 375,000 free meals a year to our community. Our job training curriculums are state certified and offer job placement assistance. We are currently accepting applications to fill our 2024 classes now. If you are interested in any of our programs, please register for an open house.

“Let’s Cook”- for Independent Special Needs Participants

“Let’s Cook” is a free program that is provided by Elijah’s Promise and Promise Culinary School for our local community in Middlesex County and Somerset County. Our goal is to educate families and individuals on how to cook healthy meals on a budget
